<h3>M.Sc Thesis (Research Assistance)</h3>

<p>
This work represents my contribution as a research assistant in an M.Sc thesis conducted at 
<b><a href="https://pust.ac.bd/">Pabna University of Science and Technology</a></b>, 
under the Department of Information and Communication Engineering.
</p>

<p>
<b>Supervisor:</b> 
<a href="https://pust.ac.bd/academic/departments/dept_teachers/dept_teachers_profile/100078">
Prof. Dr. Md. Sarwar Hosain
</a>
<br>

<b>Co-Supervisor:</b>
<a href="https://pust.ac.bd/academic/departments/dept_teachers/dept_teachers_profile/100073">
Prof. Dr. Md. Omar Faruk
</a>

</p>

<p>
The research proposes an attention-driven <b>Emoformer architecture</b>, integrating 
Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and Transformer models to effectively capture both 
local acoustic patterns and long-range temporal dependencies in speech signals.
</p>

<p><b>My Contributions:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Data preprocessing and feature extraction (MFCC, X-vectors)</li>
<li>Model implementation and training</li>
<li>Experimental evaluation and performance analysis</li>
</ul>

<p>
The system demonstrated strong performance across five emotional states 
(angry, happy, sad, surprise, neutral), highlighting its effectiveness in 
low-resource Bangla speech environments.
</p>
